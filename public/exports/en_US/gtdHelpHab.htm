Getting email under control with ActiveInbox:

Set Status

Choose a Status to action and prioritize this email to guarantee it gets completed.

Tip: Use 'Waiting On' to ensure you get a response

Tip: Use Gmail's Star, or create a new Status label (e.g. S/Next) to prioritize very important emails

Assign to Project

Group emails into manageable Projects to make them easy to find later and to focus your mind on goals.

Tip: Create sub-projects using '/' (e.g. P/Issues/Issue1)

Archive

Archive this conversation after it has been categorized to keep your inbox clean.

Then use the ActiveInbox Sidebar to track actioned conversations until completion.

Getting to Inbox Zero...

An empty inbox gives you a lovely relaxed feeling and ensures you are always on top of your important emails.

How do you achieve it? Every few hours, for each email in your inbox:

  1. Choose how to act:
    • Delete or spam it if you can
    • Reply if you can do so within 2 minutes (to move it forward)
    • If it will take longer, action and prioritize it (give it a Status) and come back when you can dedicate yourself to processing all emails
  2. Archive it and move to the next email (you can do this with just one click!).
