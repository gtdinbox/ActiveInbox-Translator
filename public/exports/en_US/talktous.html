Talk To Us

Got a problem or an idea? We can help!

We are still very busy refining the ActiveInbox experience, striving for the perfect balance of high utility and minimal features.

Everything we have done so far has been with the community's support and suggestions, and we want you to feel absolutely comfortable getting involved too. If you have any requests, opinions or just want to discuss something, then please come and get in touch.

  • Community Forum
    We like to keep as much discussion in the open as possible, as it brings together the best ideas and helps the largest number of people. We spend most of our support time in the Community Forum. Oh, and if you want to help others we'd love for you to come and share your tips and advice!
  • Email us at support@activeinboxhq.com.
    If you have a more private matter, or you want to give us a little kick to remind us about something, you can use email to reach us directly.
